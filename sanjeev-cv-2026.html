Letter

Hi

As a hands-on design leader, I currently serve as AVP – Design at HT Digital, leading multidisciplinary teams to craft user-centric, scalable digital experiences that align with both customer needs and business goals.

With over 12+ years of experience designing and scaling digital products across Fintech, Travel, Edtech, Live-streaming, Live Games & Fantasy, Native Advertising and more — I bring a strong foundation in user-centered thinking, product strategy, and organisational design.

At HT Digital I lead design across multiple verticals — News, Fintech & Affiliate — spanning products like Hindustan Times (HT), Live Mint, and Mint Money. I drive end-to-end product design across consumer and fintech ecosystems, aligning design strategy with business goals across print, digital, and credit verticals.

Prior to HT Digital, I served as Head of Design at BharatPe, a leading fintech platform in India. I led end-to-end design across both the merchant and consumer ecosystems — QR payments, UPI-based solutions, lending, and smart POS. Our platforms served over 5 million DAUs and enabled ₹600 crore (~$72M) in monthly loan disbursals. On the consumer side, I helped shape a comprehensive financial suite including UPI payments, co-branded credit cards, personal loans, and a growing wealth-tech vertical serving India's Tier 2 cities through Mutual Funds, Fixed Deposits, and Insurance.

Before BharatPe, I was a founding design member at apna.co, where I scaled the design team from scratch and helped build a job platform for India's blue & gray-collar workforce. I collaborated closely with product and business leadership to define strategy, build cross-functional alignment, and execute against quarterly OKRs.

Prior to that, I worked as Design Manager at MakeMyTrip, leading Growth, Holidays, and Design Systems. I drove the platform's expansion to the Middle East, launched multilingual Arabic experiences, and spearheaded the "Khojo Bharat" campaign under Incredible India to promote domestic travel post-pandemic.

Earlier, I spent four years in Abu Dhabi as a Founding Design Member at Algorythma, a tech initiative by ADFG. I helped launch Swoo, a live-streaming platform, and contributed to government-backed initiatives including Saal.ai and an academic portal for the Abu Dhabi Air Force — while building in-house product and design teams.

My journey began at InMobi, working on native advertising with OEMs like Samsung and LG. I was part of the founding team that built Glance.ai — now a unicorn and a cornerstone of lock screen content innovation.

I hold a Master's degree (M.Des) in UX Design from IIT Kanpur and a Bachelor's degree (B.Des) from NIFT Delhi — both premier institutions grounded in design thinking and research-led problem solving.

Now, as AVP – Design at HT Digital, I continue to seek challenges that allow me to tackle complex user problems at scale, mentor teams, and create impactful solutions that blend product thinking with empathy and craft.
